Who We Are

True CornerStone Ministry is a Christ-centered community dedicated to worship, fellowship, and serving others. We believe in growing in faith, loving one another, and reaching out to our community with compassion.

Our Vision

To raise a Christ-centered generation empowered by the Holy Spirit to transform communities, impact nations, and live in the fullness of God’s purpose.

History & Vision

Founded in 2020, True CornerStone Ministry has grown to be a beacon of hope and faith in our community. Over the years, we have expanded our outreach programs, strengthened our worship services, and fostered a loving community.

Our vision is to see lives transformed by the power of Christ, impacting families, neighborhoods, and beyond with the message of hope and grace.
